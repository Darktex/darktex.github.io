# Ceremony Palazzo Tursi
![Tursi](http://www.opengenova.org/wikilovesmonuments/files/2014/07/salone-tursi.jpg)
[Palazzo Tursi](http://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palazzo_Doria-Tursi) is at this address: [Via Garibaldi 9, 16124 Genova](https://www.google.it/maps/place/Palazzo+Doria+Tursi/@44.411282,8.932598,17z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x0000000000000000:0xb507ed5992cbe319?hl=it).

The wedding ceremony will be held there at 11.00am and should last approximately half an hour.

### Directions for people coming from the East


- The highway exit is "Genova Est" (East Genova)
- You can park here: ([link Google Maps](https://www.google.it/maps/dir/44.4381216,8.9388619/Autopark+Piccapietra+%28SP+A.%29,+Largo+San+Giuseppe,+Genova,+GE/@44.4248789,8.9239831,14z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m9!4m8!1m0!1m5!1m1!1s0x12d343e86b6ec9b3:0xa5ad62b945c6aac8!2m2!1d8.936763!2d44.408014!3e0)). It's 2.20€ per hour.
-  Walk to Palazzo Tursi ([link Google Maps](https://www.google.it/maps/dir/Autopark+Piccapietra+%28SP+A.%29,+Largo+S.+Giuseppe,+16121+Genova/Palazzo+Doria+Tursi,+Via+Garibaldi,+Genova/@44.409648,8.9325858,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m14!4m13!1m5!1m1!1s0x12d343e86b6ec9b3:0xa5ad62b945c6aac8!2m2!1d8.936763!2d44.408014!1m5!1m1!1s0x12d343e6896d1cd9:0xb507ed5992cbe319!2m2!1d8.932598!2d44.411282!3e2). Google Maps says it's a 8 min walk.

- Walk to Palazzo Tursi ([link Google Maps](https://www.google.it/maps/dir/Marina+Porto+Antico+-+Parcheggio,+Molo+Ponte+Morosini,+34/1,+Genova/Palazzo+Doria+Tursi,+Via+Garibaldi,+Genova/@44.4116557,8.9273705,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m13!4m12!1m5!1m1!1s0x12d343e1874231b9:0x73cbaf2c98e7be00!2m2!1d8.926872!2d44.412445!1m5!1m1!1s0x12d343e6896d1cd9:0xb507ed5992cbe319!2m2!1d8.932598!2d44.411282)). # La Cascina degli Ulivi

La [Cascina degli Ulivi](http://www.cascinadegliulivi.it/index.php/it/) can be found at this address:

[Strada della Mazzola 14, 15067, Novi Ligure](https://www.google.it/maps/place/Agriturismo+Cascina+Degli+Ulivi+Terranera+Soc.Coop.+Agricola/@44.74559,8.77234,17z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x0000000000000000:0x07307bfffa8b7b8a?hl=it)
