#About this website

I use it as a blog, and I use it to keep my notes in the cloud for myself (and share them to whoever may find them useful). I also like writing guides and doing talks about what I do, and they are all here for everyone to use, modify and/or redistribute freely as long as I am given due credit[^license] :-)

Speaking of giving due credit: this website is hosted on [GitHub Pages](http://pages.github.com/) and most of its content is written in [Markdown](http://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/) thanks to the great [Jekyll project](http://jekyllrb.com/).

On the top of that, I use the [So-simple theme](https://github.com/mmistakes/so-simple-theme/) for Jekyll.

My text editor of choice for this website is Sublime Text 2 and I use the following plugins:

- MarkdownEditing for syntax coloring and stuff: <https://github.com/ttscoff/MarkdownEditing>
- SublimeJekyll just to have a button to click to create a new post (yeah, lazy): <https://github.com/dnfehren/SublimeJekyll>
- WordCount (guess what it does): <https://github.com/SublimeText/WordCount>
